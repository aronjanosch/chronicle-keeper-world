---
name: Flesh out a character
description: Worldbuilding questions for a person — drive, voice, relationships, secrets, role in play. Use when the user wants to develop or deepen an NPC, PC, or other character page.
kinds: [npc, character, pc]
---

Help the user deepen a character page. Read its body first (read_page) so you build on
what is already there — never re-ask what the page answers.

How to work:
- Pick the 2–4 questions below that the page is most silent on and that matter most for
  play, and raise those. Do not dump the whole list — that is drudgery.
- These are prompts for the user, not a form to fill. Ask, let them answer in their own
  words, then offer to write it into the page (you draft, they approve). Always skippable.
- A character is built from want + obstacle + contradiction, not a stat sheet. Favour the
  details that change how they act at the table.
- Record who they know as typed relations (`ally:`, `rival:`, `serves: "[[...]]"`) so the
  graph and Relations panel pick them up. Check page_kinds for the kind's infobox fields.

Questions to draw from:

**Drive**
- What do they want most right now, and what are they willing to do to get it?
- What do they fear losing? What would break them?
- A belief or principle they will not cross — and the temptation that tests it?

**Voice and surface**
- How do they speak — cadence, verbal tics, what they never say aloud?
- What does someone notice first about them? A tell when they lie or are afraid?
- How do they dress and carry themselves, and what does it signal vs. hide?

**Past**
- Where are they from, and what shaped them — a loss, a debt, a betrayal, a triumph?
- The thing in their past they would pay to keep buried?

**Relationships**
- Who do they love, owe, resent, or serve? Who wants them dead?
- How do they treat people below, beside, and above them?

**Role in the story**
- Why would the party meet them, and what do they offer or demand?
- A secret that recontextualises them if revealed? (Consider a `[!secret]` callout.)
- How might they change over the campaign?
